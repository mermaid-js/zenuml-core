Privacy

Your diagrams stay yours

ZenUML renders in your browser. The library itself does not phone home, track you, or transmit your diagram source.

The library

The open-source @zenuml/core library runs entirely client-side. When you render a diagram, your text is parsed and drawn locally in the browser or host application. The library does not send your diagram source, inputs, or personal data to any ZenUML server.

Hosted surfaces

Products that embed ZenUML — the Live Editor, the Confluence app, IDE plugins — each have their own privacy terms set by their host platform. Review the relevant provider's policy for details about accounts, storage, and analytics on those surfaces.

This website

This marketing site serves static pages and fonts from Google Fonts. It sets no advertising cookies. If we add analytics later, we will update this page first.
