[ PRIVACY ]

Your diagrams stay yours.

ZenUML renders in your browser. The library itself does not phone home, track you, or transmit your diagram source.

The library

The open-source @zenuml/core library runs entirely client-side. When you render a diagram, your text is parsed and drawn locally in the browser or host application. The library does not send your diagram source, inputs, or personal data to any ZenUML server.

Hosted surfaces

Products that embed ZenUML — the Live Editor at app.zenuml.com, the Confluence app, IDE plugins — each have their own privacy terms set by their host platform. Review the relevant provider's policy for details about accounts, storage, and analytics on those surfaces.

This website

This site serves static pages. It loads the JetBrains Mono webfont from Google Fonts, and the home page additionally loads the @zenuml/core bundle from the jsDelivr CDN to render its live demo in your browser — like any web request, those fetches disclose your IP address to the respective CDN. The site itself sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and serves no ads. If we add analytics later, we will update this page first.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@zenuml.com. This plain-language policy will be replaced with a full legal document before public launch.
